About DTU Volunteer Platform

Connecting Duy Tan University students with meaningful volunteer opportunities to develop skills, fulfill social responsibility, and create lasting community impact.

The Challenge We're Solving

1

Lack of Centralized Information

Students struggle to find volunteer activities as information is scattered across social media and unofficial channels.

2

Manual Registration Process

Non‑specialized forms like Google Forms make it difficult to track registration status and manage applications.

3

Limited Recognition

Students lack a reliable system to verify project transparency and receive recognition for their contributions.

The Impact of This Problem

Suboptimal Resource Utilization

Many students want to volunteer but struggle to find suitable activities, leading to wasted potential and missed opportunities for community impact.

Low Operational Efficiency

Organizations spend excessive time on manual administration and management, reducing their ability to focus on actual volunteer work.

Risk to Trust

Without transparent reporting systems, both volunteers and sponsors may hesitate to participate due to concerns about how contributions are used.

Our Solution

A comprehensive platform designed specifically for DTU students to discover, register, and track their volunteer journey

Centralized Platform

All volunteer opportunities in one place, making it easy to find activities that match your interests and schedule.

Easy Registration

Simple, streamlined registration process. No more scattered Google Forms or manual paperwork.

Track Your Impact

Monitor your volunteer hours, activities, and achievements. Get official certificates for your contributions.

Transparent Results

See the real impact of volunteer projects through verified metrics and community feedback.

Why We Built This Platform

Project Necessity

The market lacks a specialized, effective platform to connect DTU students with volunteer activities. This project fills that critical gap.

Leveraging Resources

DTU has thousands of young, dynamic students representing an important social resource. This platform effectively mobilizes that potential.
